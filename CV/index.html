Biography

Xuyang Cao is a third year Ph.D. candidate in Beijing Jiaotong University, supervised by professor Houjin Chen and associate professor Yanfen Li. He also works closely with professor Yahui Peng. His research area include Medical Image Analysis and Machine Learning.

His current research interest is medical image segmentation algorithms, especially semi-supervised segmentation.

Projects

2019-Present Study on Breast Mass Detection, Classification and Parameter Measurement Methods using ABVS Images,  The National Natural Science Fund,   Participant

This project mainly focus on detection, segmentation, classification, and 3D reconstruction of breast tumors in ultrasound modality. In this project, I mainly study the segmentation of ultrasound images from the perspective of 2D and 3D.
2018-Present Research on Detection and Classification of Lung Masses Based on MRI Images,   Participant

This project is to study the method of lung mass segmentation, multi-modual lung mass feature extraction, multi-modual feature fusion, benign and malignant classification of lung mass. I mainly study the 2D MRI image segmentation of the mass in this project.
2017-2018 High Speed Train Gear Defect Detection Based on Computer Vision,   Horizontal Scientific Research Project,   Participant

Based on image acquisition and recognition technology, this project developed an automatic detection system for surface pitting of drum tooth gears of high-speed train.I was mainly responsible for the developing of defect detection algorithms and the implementation of software systems.
2016-2017 Geometric Parameters Measurement of an Overhead Line System,   Participant

This Project provided a method and a device for measuring the geometric parameters of an overhead line system by using scale factors and frame differences.I was mainly responsible for the previous algorithm simulation, and participated in the hardware structure design work.
